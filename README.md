# EVCharge Pro 

This is a realistic calculator for finding the best price possible to charge your car near you.

I always wonder how much it would cost between charging near me overnight for extra time or finding a fast charger along the way for a long road trip. I know that there are a TON of off-peak charging rates and other shenanigans. I decided to include as much as I can so that I know not ONLY how much I will have to pay for my charging, but also how much it would cost including parking, another one of my issues. When I'm going to Philly, in one trip, do I find a charger out of the way, do I find a garage in the city center, do I just settle for a little fast charging to get me back home? This way, although it's community based, I am able to stop having to worry about these things and just decide something with a few button presses. 

I felt like this is a major gap within the already difficult-to-navigate electric vehicle charging landscape. This is just one way I want to make it easier for people like me to find out how much they are actually saving instead of buying a Prius. 

Along with my Perfect Car Picker app, I think these are great additions for new EV buyers as well looking for a cheap-to-own vehicle who also want to support the environment.

Hope this helps people, feedback is appreciated.
